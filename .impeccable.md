## Design Context

### Users
Emily (accountant) and business owners of a concrete flooring company. Used during work hours in an office context to manage invoices, payroll, and reports. Power users who need density and clarity, not onboarding.

### Brand Personality
**Precise. Industrial. No-nonsense.**
Apex Concrete installs real physical things. The interface should feel like a well-machined tool — heavy, reliable, intentional. Not soft, not playful.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first (construction workers, dimly-lit offices, screen-heavy work). Orange accent (#f97316) as brand signal. Steel blue as structural color.
Anti-references: generic SaaS pastel dashboards, glassmorphism, anything that looks like a startup landing page.
References: industrial control panels, financial terminals, machined metal surfaces.

### Design Principles
1. **Density over decoration** — every pixel earns its place
2. **Number-forward** — monetary values and SF quantities are the content; design around them
3. **Mechanical precision** — tight spacing, sharp edges where appropriate, consistent rhythm
4. **Dark backgrounds, sharp contrast** — light text on steel surfaces, orange accent used sparingly
5. **Functional motion only** — transitions exist to show state change, nothing decorative
